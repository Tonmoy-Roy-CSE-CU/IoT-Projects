Autonomous Obstacle Avoidance Robot

A 27 cm × 25 cm robot powered by the ESP8266 NodeMCU, using four DC motors (paired as left/right) for navigation. It features an HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor on an SG90 servo for 140° scanning (20°/160°), enabling precise obstacle avoidance with 90° turns and ~7–10 cm reverse maneuvers. Includes a custom circuit diagram and demo video.
